## Relaxation and comfort

**Located in the heart of Zihuatanejo, with easy access to shops, restaurants, banks and just 5 minutes from the Beach**, Zihua Express is the best option for your stay in Zihuatanejo, with an unbeatable combination of quality, comfort, service , attention and fair prices.

Zihua Express has touches of traditional architecture and its own details that generate a warm, calm and relaxed atmosphere, making it ideal for relaxing and enjoying family vacations.

**Zihua Express has 34 comfortable rooms.** Each one is equipped with air conditioning, fan, cable TV and free wireless Internet coverage, in addition to having a pool and children's pool.
