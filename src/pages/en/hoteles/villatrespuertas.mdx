## Stay in Ixtapa

Enjoy the wonderful sun of Ixtapa and our original swimming pool, or let us organize various activities for you such as diving, sport fishing, golf, tennis or motorcycle rental. Relax in our charming Villas, **near the sea, at the Golf Club and with an unbeatable location**.

Villas Tres Puertas is a concept that combines the comfort of a small hotel with the exclusivity of a private villa.

Live for a few days the experience of an environment of tropical vegetation and enjoy the personal details of its owners.

**Our Villas**, for up to four people, **are equipped with kitchen**, living area, king size bed, two single beds, ceiling fans and terrace.
