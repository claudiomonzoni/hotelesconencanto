## Stay in front of Zihuatanejo Bay

**The Hotel Villas el Morro has a spectacular view of the Zihuatanejo bay** and the open sea, we guarantee the best view of this beautiful bay but we are not on the beach. We like to smile and make the people who visit us smile.

The hotel is located on the crest of a hill in Zihuatanejo Bay, 65 meters above sea level, a forty-minute flight from Mexico City, **ten minutes WALKING from the center of Zihuatanejo**, 15 Minutes walk from the main beach and Contramar beach and 15 km/9 miles from the international and local airport.

It has spacious **suites, with private terraces**, its main attractions are the views from the villas up to 270 degrees around, where you can contemplate the entire bay and the open sea, from the pool or in your Jacuzzi in your room. this comfortable, hospitable, private, and luxurious Hotel.
