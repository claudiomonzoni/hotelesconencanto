## Atmosphere of hospitality and warmth

**We are a family-owned hotel founded in the 80s to serve travelers** who want to discover the Ixtapa-Zihuatanejo coast.

We are part of a group of hoteliers in Zihuatanejo, personally serving the needs of their guests. We strive to meet your expectations and you have to return to this Mexican Pacific paradise.

Since the creation of Ixtapa-Zihuatanejo as a tourist destination, we imagined a hotel with the goal of serving customers while creating an atmosphere of hospitality and warmth. We welcome you to the Hotel Posada Riscal, where "our house is your home."

**We welcome you with open arms!**
